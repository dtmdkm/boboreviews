How to apply Store coupon codes

Step 1: Find your Store Coupons, discount codes on this page or Boboreviews and click "GET CODE" button to view the code, then click "Copy" and the coupons, discount codes will be copied to your phone's or computer's clipboard.

Step 3: During checkout, find the text "Promo Code" or "Discount Code" and paste your Store coupons, discount codes in step 1 to this box. Click "Apply" and your savings for Store will be applied.
